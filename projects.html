Flagship projects

The work that best demonstrates the crossover between clinical genomics research and production-grade engineering.

SEMseeker

R · AGPL-v3 · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.5095416 · basis of the PhD thesis

An R package for Epigenome-Wide Association Studies (EWAS) based on stochastic epimutation. It underpins the SEM methodology used in the first-author papers and is the strongest demonstration of research and code delivered in the same commit.

Repository → Zenodo DOI →
REWASDNA methylationepimutation

ctdR

R

Enrichment analysis (ORA and GSEA) of chemical-gene interactions from the Comparative Toxicogenomics Database. A toxicogenomics enrichment tool that pairs directly with the Agent Orange and environmental-exposure research.

Repository →
Rtoxicogenomicsenrichment

spendif-ai

Python · offline-first, privacy-safe

A personal-finance ledger that aggregates bank statements (CSV and XLSX) into a single ledger with hybrid deterministic and LLM categorization. A demonstration of privacy-first data products, currently launching on Product Hunt.
